Overview

Devised by Brian Silverman, Brian's Brain is a two-dimentional Cellular Automaton that exists in an infinite square grid where each cell has three states: On, Dying or Off.

Each time step the cells consider all 8 adjacent neighbours and follow the rules:

  1. All Off cells turn on if they have two neighbours that are On
  2. All On cells turn to dying
  3. All Dying cells turn off

Visualisation

Visualising Brian's Brain can be done with a grid of square cells that have one of three colours, depending on state.
